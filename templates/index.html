Setup

  • Robot: ESP32-C3 over Ethernet (LAN). PC and robot on the same network.
  • Camera: Join SJ4000 Wi-Fi. Default camera IP is 192.168.1.254.
  • SJ4000 MJPEG: Switch camera to Photo mode, then Start Feed.
  • SJ4000 RTSP: Switch camera to Video mode. Requires FFmpeg on this PC.
  • Drive: W forward, S back, A/D turn.
